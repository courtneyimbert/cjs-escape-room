CJ's Escape Room Rules

  1. Stay in the game area
    The game is the living room, dining room, and hallway on the first floor.
  2. Don't make a mess
    All clues are out in the open or simple to uncover. You do not have to open drawers, dig through baskets, or move furniture. Do not break, throw, or hit anything. If you aren't sure, check with CJ!
  3. Ask for hints if you need it!
    CJ will provide you a hint if you're stuck.
  4. Have fun!
